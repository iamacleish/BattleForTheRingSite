About

Ian has been designing and playing games for most of his life. He has experience in level design, camera controllers including virtual reality, 3D modeling, gameplay mechanics and more. In addition to multiple simulations such as a virtual reality bike experience, Ian has worked on several games. These include Suddenly Words!, and Bar Crawl: A Night to Forget, both of which are available on app stores. Currently, Ian is working on a visual novel and card game called Boffer Quest.
