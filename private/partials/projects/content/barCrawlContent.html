Bar Crawl: A Night to Forget

Date

Link

Drunkenly fight your way through a bar using a control scheme that combines the puzzle style of Puzzles and Dragons, with the erratic movement and hilarity of Twitch Plays Pokemon. The game starts with the player punching a biker in the face which starts a bar brawl. The player solves 1 round of the puzzle, doing those actions in the order that the puzzle was cleared. The other people in the bar respond, and then its the players turn again. This continues until the player is knocked out.
